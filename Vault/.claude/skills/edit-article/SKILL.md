# Edit Article

## Workflow

### 1. Section the article

Divide the article into sections based on its headings. Think about the main points each section makes.

Treat information as a directed acyclic graph — pieces of information depend on other pieces. Check that the order of sections respects these dependencies. A section that relies on a concept should come after the section that introduces it.

Confirm the sections with the user before editing.

### 2. Edit each section

For each section in order:

- Rewrite to improve clarity, coherence, and flow
- Maximum 240 characters per paragraph
- Cut anything that doesn't advance the section's main point
- Preserve the author's voice — improve structure, not personality

### 3. Review for consistency

After all sections are edited:
- Check that transitions between sections are smooth
- Verify the opening and closing are strong
- Confirm the article delivers on whatever its headline promises

Present the edited version. Ask for feedback section by section if the article is long.
