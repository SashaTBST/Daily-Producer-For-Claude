# /tax-return — Reference

## Income Tax Brackets — FY2025 (Stage 3 cuts from 1 July 2024)
| Income | Rate |
|---|---|
| $0 – $18,200 | 0% (tax-free threshold) |
| $18,201 – $45,000 | 19c per $1 over $18,200 |
| $45,001 – $135,000 | 32.5c per $1 over $45,000 |
| $135,001 – $190,000 | 37c per $1 over $135,000 |
| $190,001+ | 45c per $1 over $190,000 |

Plus 2% Medicare levy on all taxable income.
LITO: max $700 at income ≤$37,500, phases out. Auto-applied by ATO.

## D-Items — Work-Related Deduction Categories
| Code | Category | Key Rules |
|---|---|---|
| D1 | Work-related car | 88c/km FY2025, max 5,000km OR logbook |
| D2 | Work-related travel | Overnight work travel only. Not daily commute. |
| D3 | Clothing/laundry | Protective or compulsory uniform only. No plain clothing. |
| D4 | Self-education | Course directly connected to current role only. |
| D5 | Other work-related | Home office, phone/internet (work %), tools, subscriptions |
| D7 | Interest deductions | Loan interest on income-producing investments |
| D8 | Dividend deductions | Costs of managing share/fund investments |
| D9 | Gifts and donations | DGR-registered charities only, $2 minimum, receipt required |
| D10 | Managing tax affairs | Prior year's tax agent fees only |

$300 rule: work-related expenses under $300 total (excluding car, travel, clothing, self-education) — no receipt required, but must explain basis.

## CGT Rules
- CGT event = disposal: sale, trade, gift, or using asset to purchase goods/services
- Capital gain = proceeds minus cost base (purchase price + brokerage + improvement costs)
- 50% CGT discount: asset held 12+ months before disposal
- Capital losses: must offset capital gains same FY first. Net losses carried forward.
- Crypto: every disposal is a CGT event. Staking rewards = ordinary income. Airdrops = ordinary income.
- Vanguard: Annual Tax Statement contains all required figures — download Aug–Sep.

## Working From Home — Fixed Rate (FY2025)
- 70c per hour worked from home
- Covers: electricity, gas, internet, phone, stationery, minor equipment
- Does NOT cover: depreciation of dedicated office furniture (claim separately)
- Requires: actual record of hours for full year — estimates not accepted from FY2023 onward

## Lodgment Deadlines — FY2025
- Self-lodge via myTax: 31 October 2025 (passed)
- Registered tax agent: 15 May 2026

## Personal Super Contributions (Concessional)
- Deductible if contributed before 30 June AND Notice of Intent lodged before return is filed
- Annual concessional cap: $30,000 FY2025 — includes employer (SG) contributions
- Carry-forward unused cap available if total super balance <$500,000 at 30 June of prior year

## Common Flags for Tax Agent
- Income protection insurance premiums paid personally (generally deductible)
- HECS/HELP debt — compulsory repayment calculated on taxable income
- Prior year capital losses carried forward
- Foreign income — all foreign source income must be declared
- Spouse/partner offset — may apply if partner income is low
- Depreciation schedule — if work or business assets were purchased
