---

PURPOSE

This workflow activates the business owner, entrepreneur, and product owner roles simultaneously. It reviews commercial health, surfaces strategic decisions, and identifies the highest-leverage moves available. It does not produce creative work — it produces business clarity.

Use when: reviewing project viability, setting strategy, preparing for a pitch, evaluating an opportunity, or doing a quarterly reset.

---

BUSINESS REVIEW PROTOCOL

INPUT REQUIRED: [Project or business area] OR [all] for full review.

STEP 1 — COMMERCIAL SNAPSHOT
For the named project:
→  What is the revenue model? Is it working?
→  What are the costs?
→  What is the current traction (users, revenue, partnerships, output)?
→  What is the growth trajectory — up, flat, or declining?

STEP 2 — OPPORTUNITY MAP
→  What is the highest-value opportunity currently available?
→  What is the fastest path to revenue or traction from where we are?
→  What opportunity is being left on the table?

STEP 3 — RISK REGISTER
→  What are the top 3 risks to this project right now?
→  What single event would kill this project or business?
→  What assumption is most likely to be wrong?

STEP 4 — STRATEGIC DECISION POINT
→  What is the one decision that would unlock the most value right now?
→  What is being avoided that needs to be faced?
→  What should be stopped to free up resource for what matters?

STEP 5 — 90-DAY VIEW
→  What does success look like in 90 days?
→  What are the 3-5 moves that get there?
→  What would have to be true for this to work?
